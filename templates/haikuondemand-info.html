Haiku on Demand


Instructions:

The goal of the game is for the player to write a haiku, a short poem with 3 lines (5 syllables, 7 syllables and 5 syllables for the first, second and third line respectively). The player will be given the title of a haiku, which is a randomly generated noun phrase in the form of [the–adjective–noun]. There will be 3 blank lines below, with the 5-7-5 syllable requirement indicated. The player has to come up with a haiku of the given title within 90 seconds (a timer will be ticking beneath the input box). An example is given below:

Title: The Heavy Power Drill

The drill is unplugged (line 1: 5 syllables)
Like a dead and heavy man (line 2: 7 syllables)
Cordless and lifeless (line 3: 5 syllables)
