Wicked Proverbs


Instructions:

The player will be given two separate words and a randomly generated prompt, such as "My grandfather used to say...", and will have to (i) come up with a proverb, and (ii) explain the usage of the newly created proverb within 90 seconds (a timer will be clicking beneath the box). Please make sure your input is a complete sentence/complete sentences rather than fragments. An example is given below:

Words given: moustache, cotton candy

Proverb: Cotton candy on a moustache attracts bees from afar.

Explanation: If you have a good idea, however weird, makes it as visible as possible and it will attract investors.
