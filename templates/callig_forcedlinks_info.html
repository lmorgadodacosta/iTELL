Forced Links


Instructions:

The player will be given two separate words, A and B. The player has to come up with connecting words or phrases that would link A to B. After each input, the player can click on the "Add Word" button to add another slot. There are no limits in the number of links in-between the two given words. There is, however, a time limit of 40 seconds to finish the task. It is essentially a game of association to a fixed destination.

Prompt: moustache      ..?..    cotton candy

Links: Moustache      hair      fluffy      cotton candy
