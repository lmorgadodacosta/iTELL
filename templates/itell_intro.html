iTELL


iTELL is a suite of applications looking into applying deep computational parsers to intelligent Technology Enhanced Language Learning environments. iTELL includes several applications, for both English and Mandarin Chinese, exploring how to leverage the broad linguistic knowledge available to deep computational parsers and apply it to pedagogical settings.
